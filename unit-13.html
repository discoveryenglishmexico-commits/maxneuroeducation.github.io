📖 La Narrativa

El Itinerario Europeo

The Williams are planning their trip to foreign countries. They will visit several countries throughout Europe. They sure are eager to get going. First they will check through the internet a travel agency that can offer the best options for them.

They would like to go to Spain, France, Italy and to wrap it up in Dutchland. They're working hard as a whole to obtain their objective. They would like to be able to travel by April of next year on Easter week's vacation and enjoy the spring in Europe.

At the Sunshine Travel Agency the packages are excellent, the flight to Spain is not so expensive and from there they could travel by train to the other places they wish to visit, which makes it even more exciting. Although they plan to check with another agency to make the choice.

Comprensión Estratégica

📚 Grammar Review: Conditionals & Present Perfect

Conditional Forms

Used to imagine events in certain conditions. Real events (First), imaginary (Second), or imagined past (Third).

If we finish early, we will go out for lunch. (Possible)
If we had time, we would visit our friends. (Imaginary)
If we had gone to New York, we would have visited the exhibition. (Past Imagined)

Present Perfect

Connects a past moment to the present. Covers duration from past until now, life experience, or recent past events influencing the present.

I've lived in New York for twenty years.
I've visited every state in the country.
I've just had lunch.
