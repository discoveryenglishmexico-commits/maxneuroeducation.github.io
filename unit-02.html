🎓 Diálogo: Graduation Day

Escucha · Repite · Memoriza

Teacher: Congratulations David, I heard that you'll graduate from middle school education this week.

David: Thank you, I'm really happy of finishing my middle school education. Now I'll begin my high school education.

Teacher: You're not a little child anymore; you're a young adult now. Your new teachers will expect you to be responsible and mature.

David: I know. It's time for me to stop being childish. I should be serious about studying.

Teacher: If you study and get good grades in high school, your parents will be very proud of you.
