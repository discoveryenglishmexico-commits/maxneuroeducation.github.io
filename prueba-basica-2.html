Prueba de Conocimientos Básicos 1

Unidad: At the High School

🔄 1. Convierte en Preguntas

Convierte las afirmaciones en preguntas. La pregunta en español conviértela en inglés.

Is this the classroom's globe? Yes it is.
Is this a sharpener? No it isn't. It's the classroom's globe.
Are these the classmates? Yes they are.
Are these people teachers? No they aren't. They're the class mates.

🎯 2. Pronombres Demostrativos

Aplica correctamente this/that/these/those distinguiendo entre plural y singular.

Are those Joshua and Peter? Yes they are.
Are those Jimmy and Natasha? No they aren't. They're Joshua and Peter.
Is that my friend Claudia? Yes she is.
Is that my English teacher? No she isn't. She's my friend Claudia.
Are these computers? Yes they are.
Are these tablets? No they aren't. They're computers.
Are those tablets? Yes they are.
Are those computers? No they aren't. They're tablets.

📦 3. There is / There are

Completa con la forma correcta y agrega respuesta afirmativa/negativa.

Is there a student in the classroom? Yes there is.
Is there a janitor in the classroom? No there isn't. There's a student in the classroom.
Are there students in the classroom? Yes there are.
Are there janitors in the classroom? No there aren't. There are students in the classroom.
Is there a math problem on the board? Yes there is.
Is there a history question on the board? No there isn't. There's a math problem on the board.
Are there math problems on the board? Yes there are.
Are there history questions on the board? No there aren't. There are math problems on the board.

📍 4. Critical Thinking & Preposiciones

Responde usando In/On correctamente. Respuestas completas requeridas.

They're in the classroom.
It's on the bookcase.
He's in the English class.
It's in the school.
It's on the teacher's desk.
They're in the classroom.
