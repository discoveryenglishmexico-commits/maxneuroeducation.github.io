🇪🇸 2. Tourist Attractions in Spain

Explore the noteworthy attractions the Williams visited in Spain.

🏰 The Alcázar of Segovia

A stone fortification located in the old city of Segovia, Spain. The Alcázar was originally built as a fortress but has served as a royal palace, a state prison, a Royal Artillery College and a military academy since then. The castle is one of the inspirations for Walt Disney's Cinderella Castle.

🏖️ The Costa del Sol

A region in the south of Spain, in the autonomous community of Andalusia Province of Malaga. Today the region is a tourist destination of world renown.

🎨 The Prado

Spain's national museum and one of the best in Europe. Remarkable collection of European paintings.

🌳 Park of the Pleasant Retreat (El Retiro)

One of the largest parks of the city of Madrid, Spain. The park belonged to the Spanish Monarchy until the late 19th century, when it became a public park. It's a magnificent park, filled with beautiful sculptures and monuments, galleries, a peaceful lake and host to a variety of events. The park is entirely surrounded by the present-day city.

🇮🇹 4. Tourist Attractions in Italy

Explore the ancient wonders of Rome.

🏛️ Ostia Antica

One of Rome's underrated gems but one of the best tourist attractions. It contains the strikingly well safeguarded remains of Rome's ancient port. It's fascinating and just dazzling to explore.

🏟️ The Colosseum

An absolute must for any tour of Rome. In its time it was the largest amphitheatre of the Roman Empire, where gladiators, criminals and lions alike fought for their lives.

⛲ The Trevi Fountain

An iconic monument in the city. It was designed and completed in the 18th century. Tourists come to the fountain in order to throw a coin, which is, according to a local legend, supposed to bring good luck.

🪜 The Spanish Steps

One of the city's top attractions, the flight of 138 stairs is the biggest in Europe. They are topped by the "Trinita dei Monti" church, and below there is the large "Piazza di Spagna", which is also a major shopping destination in the city, and hosts several designer boutiques.

🇩🇪 5. Tourist Attractions in Germany

Discover Berlin's most impressive landmarks.

🏛️ The Reichstag

A place in which one can look out from the building's glass dome to get a bird's eye view of the hustle and bustle in the city.

🏛️ The Gendarmenmarkt

One of the most spectacular squares in Berlin, located close to Friedrichstraße, Berlin's exclusive shopping street in the central Mitte district. Three of the most impressive examples of architecture in the capital city are to be found here: the (Konzerthaus) Concert House and the German and French Cathedrals.

🛍️ The Friedrichstraße

The main point for culture and shopping as a street in central Berlin. This downtown area is known for its stylish landholdings. Place where the Williams relished a lot due to its wide array of emporiums and restaurants. (Shop, shop, shop/chow, chow, chow)

🏰 Charlottenburg Palace

The largest palace in Berlin, and the only surviving royal residence in the city. Its gorgeous gardens are a major blast as a tourist attraction.

🧱 Berlin Wall Memorial & Brandenburg Gate

To wrap up their trip to the old continent, they had a slight walk through the Berlin's wall memorial and a stroll through the "Brandenburg Gate", Berlin's signature attraction.

📚 12. Grammar Review: Phrasal Verbs with "Off"

Review the key phrasal verbs meaning "departing".

🚀 Drop off (Separable)

To take someone, or to deliver something, to a place (usually in a car)

Examples:
On your way to work, could you drop off the books at the library?
I'm going in the direction of your office, so I can drop you off on the way.

🚪 Get off (Inseparable)

To depart for a journey or to leave a place to go somewhere

Examples:
I want to get off early tomorrow morning so we can get to our destination in the afternoon.
I have to get off to the office now.

🛣️ Head off / Set off (Inseparable)

To start a journey

Examples:
Every year in August, we head off to the mountains in Vancouver.
Where are you heading off this summer?

👋 See off (Separable)

To go to the airport, etc. in order to say goodbye to someone
To force someone to leave a place

Examples:
I am going to the airport to see my husband off.
Don't try to force your way into my house, or I will ask my dogs to see you off.

✈️ Take off (Inseparable)

(For airplanes, birds, or insects) To begin to fly

Examples:
I like watching airplanes take off.
Look at that baby eagle trying to take off!
