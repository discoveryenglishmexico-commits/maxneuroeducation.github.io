📖 Reading: A Brand New Used Car

🎙️ Hey, hey fellows, it's me again Eddy Williams

I'm here to share a whole new experience, my folks are actually going to acquire a car for me, because I've finally hooked them up, about how necessary it is for my daily come and go. I've been telling them that if they were to buy me a ride, I would have more time to study.

But now the thing is, which ride I can get with only $3,000.00 grand, obviously I'm going to go to a car dealer with my old man, because he has way more experience than me, if we are talking about cars.

Pops learn to drive when he was 12 years old, gramps used to sit him on his lap and let him hold on to the steering wheel as they were driving down the roadway, I remember the first time that I heard that story I was really amazed, before that, I used to think that gramps was cool but after that, to me he is the coolest.

I had to go to a driving school to learn how to drive because pops was too grim with me when he was teaching me and that only made me get excessively edgy, so because of that I decided to change. I guess it was a good call because in the driving school they helped me with the exam at the DMV, which made it easier to get my driver's permit.

So I'm all set to start on the hunt for my new car, to tell you the truth I'm so anxious to get started on doing so. The only bad part about this deal is that I'm going to be my sister's chauffeur, it sucks but it'll be worth it.

Now if you were in my place which kind of car would you recommend me to get? Obviously I want a sports car but I know that those kind of cars eat up a lot of gas. I know decisions, decisions.
